About RahatSetu

Citizens Get Help. NGOs Respond Fast.

  • RahatSetu is a unified relief coordination portal connecting citizens in need with NGOs across Maharashtra.
  • File an urgent report — for food, water, shelter, or medical emergencies — without any sign-in required.
  • Reports are routed directly into the NGO operations dashboard and prioritised by district severity.
  • NGO volunteers and coordinators can log in to track allocations, manage deployments, and view live heatmaps.

Quick Route

Citizens: File your urgent report using the Emergency Submission link at the top. NGO coordinators: log in below to access the full operations portal.
